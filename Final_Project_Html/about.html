About Us
The building that is now Brandon's Bakery has been in the same location for three generations and shall will be passed down to next kin. We are surrounded by water and right beside Jeremiah's Butcher Shop. What could be better than fresh baked bread? Fresh meat to go on top of it!
We offer many baked goods from muffins and cakes, to brioche and biscuits. All of these are baked fresh every morning by the grandson of Nana Baker. On top of all of these baked treats, we also offer tours around the shop and some of the town. We do tasting every week, just in case you are thinking about ordering some catering or maybe even a wedding cake.
