About Us

This bakery was created by my grandmother "Nana Baker." Every morning she baked fresh bread and pastries for the people of the town. Eventually she came up with the idea to sell these pastries to help raise money for her daughters college tuition in the future. So she saved up her money until she had enough to buy a small cottage. This cottage became the epicenter for baked goods. People came from far and wide to have these treats. When she was getting up in her age she decided that it was a good idea to pass it down to her daughter. Her daughter now had a degree in business, with the help of her mother. This helped her with some advertisement. Even though it was passed down another generation to me, nothing has changed about the quality of Nana's treats. It was her wishes to change the name to the next child to inherit.
